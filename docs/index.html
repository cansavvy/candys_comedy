About

Candy is a standup comedian based in Baltimore whose work is personal, random, and always a little unhinged. She’s performed everywhere from open mics to funerals and her therapy appointments — and whether you’re 4 or 40, she’ll find a way to make you laugh. Her humor tackles parenting chaos, marriage, and growing up in the Midwest, all delivered with the unearned confidence of a dorky mom who has absolutely no business being this enthusiastic about it.
